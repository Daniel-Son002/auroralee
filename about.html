02. About

Hi! My name is Aurora Lee, and I am currently a candidate in the University of California, Irvine (UCI) Master of Arts in Teaching + Credential program for Multiple Subjects. I graduated from Harvard in May of 2025 with a B.A. in Integrative Biology and minor in Educational Studies.

As a UCI MAT Multiple Subject candidate, I have gained experience student teaching in elementary schools in Orange County, CA while learning about research-based teaching practices, inquiry-based learning, and integrating disciplines to support student growth. I have always loved teaching and find so much joy in building strong student-teacher relationships that keep me smiling every day in the classroom. As an educator, I work hard to include all students and create a positive, supportive, and academically challenging learning environment. I hope to continue to support students in achieving their individual goals and spread my love of learning.
